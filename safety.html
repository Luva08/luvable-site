Your Safety is Our Priority

🛡️ Automated Moderation

Our AI-powered moderation system automatically detects and prevents inappropriate content, ensuring a safe environment for everyone.

⚠️ Warning System

Users receive warnings for minor violations of community guidelines. Multiple warnings may result in temporary or permanent bans.

🚫 Instant Bans

Serious violations including harassment, graphic content, or abusive behavior result in immediate account suspension.

🔒 Privacy Protection

  • We don't share your personal information with third parties
  • Chat messages are moderated but not stored long-term
  • Payment information is securely processed by Stripe
  • You can request account deletion at any time

📝 Community Guidelines

  • Treat everyone with respect and kindness
  • No bullying, harassment, or discriminatory language
  • No sharing of personal contact information
  • No solicitation or commercial promotion
  • No graphic, violent, or inappropriate content

📞 Report Issues

If you encounter any problems or violations, please contact our support team immediately. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly.

🔍 How We Moderate

Our moderation system uses advanced filters to detect inappropriate language, patterns, and behavior. This helps us maintain a positive environment while allowing genuine conversations to flourish.
